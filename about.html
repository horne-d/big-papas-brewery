Big Papa's Brewery

Changing the game forever.

The Beginning...

Founded in 2008, this was a small countryside dream to make a smooth beer for locals to enjoy. Who would of though just 9 years on pubs up and down the country would have us on tap.

This started with a couple of guys dream to make there own beer out of there farm house in the country. A way for us to brew there own beer or ale, and always have this available. We soon realised there product was better than expected, and took it to our local in the hopes to sell this on a small scale. It all soon kicked off, from a few barrels a month to a few barrels per week made us realise that perhaps we could sell on a larger scale. We approached more pubs and bars in the area, and before we knew it, Big Papa's Brewery was born!

Take a look behind the scenes of our new brewery.

Today...

Today we have our brew on tap in over 500 pubs and bars across the UK, and will shortly be breaking out into Europe. To achieve this, we have our new state of the art brewery where the product is made.

We love to focus on our customers needs also, so we take suggestions on different flavours regularly and create themed ales based on occasions. Our Pumkin Beer has been our best seller, and this was brought out initially as an annual halloween ale, but we could not stop there. This became a regular on our line and is still our number one to this day.
